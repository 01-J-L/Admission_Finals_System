About Padre Garcia Polytechnic College

Discover our commitment to excellence in education and community development.

Vision, Mission, & Core Values

"GARCIANO AKO, DISIPLINADO, MABUTING TAO, IPINAGMAMALAKI KO."

Vision

PGPC is a premier local higher learning institution in Region IV producing globally competitive and excellent professionals and leader's instrumental to community development and nation building.

Mission

As a higher learning institution in Region IV, PGPC is committed to produce globally competitive professionals and leaders with industry-based knowledge, skills, and values that will enable them achieve their professional growth and total human development.

Core Values

  • P - Professional Development
  • G - God-centered Community
  • P - Progressive Community
  • C - Commitment to Excellence

Meet People

At Padre Garcia Polytechnic College, you're not just joining an institution — you're becoming part of a dynamic and supportive community. Meet passionate educators, dedicated staff, and ambitious students who are all working together to shape a brighter future. Whether you're engaging with student leaders, collaborating with faculty, the PGPC experience is one of connection, inspiration, and shared growth. Our college takes pride in cultivating a culture of inclusivity, mentorship, and opportunity where everyone is encouraged to thrive.

Doc. Niño Javinal Viñas

President

The President of Padre Garcia Polytechnic College, Doc. Niño Javinal Viñas provides overall leadership and strategic vision, focusing on ensuring academic excellence and the relevance of its technical-vocational programs. He is responsible for managing the college's comprehensive operations, resources, and fostering student development, while also spearheading efforts to strengthen community engagement and industry partnerships. Leveraging his doctoral expertise, Doc. Viñas guides PGPC in its mission to deliver quality education and contribute significantly to the socio-economic progress of Padre Garcia and the surrounding region.
