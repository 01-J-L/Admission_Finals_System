Admission Procedure

Step 1: Create Account/Login
New applicants must create a student account. Existing applicants can log in.

Step 2: Fill Application Form
Complete all required sections of the online application form accurately.

Step 3: Upload Documents
Upload scanned copies of necessary documents, including a recent 2x2 photo.

Step 4: Submit Application
Review your application thoroughly before final submission.

Step 5: Check Status
You will be notified via email about your application status. You can also check the "My Application" section on the portal.
