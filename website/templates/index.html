PGPC Fire and Earthquake Drill last March 15, 2025

March 15, 2025

The fire and earthquake drill is in compliance with Republic Act 9514, otherwise known as the Fire Code of the Philippines of 2008, which mandates educating people on fire safety procedures and protocols. With preparedness in PGPC as our priority, our PGPC Emergency Response Team actively participated in the drill to enhance response capabilities and ensure readiness in the event of an earthquake. Disaster preparedness starts with awareness and action! Let’s continue to stay vigilant and proactive in building a safer and more resilient community.
